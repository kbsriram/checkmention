Checkmentions

This site lets you test your webmention implementation on your indieweb site, and whether it robustly detects certain types of XSS attacks.

Indieauth with your url and choose a page on your site. I will send it one good webmention, and others that try to embed some XSS in it. You can then check back on your page to see how it handles them.
